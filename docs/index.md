Javier Iparraguirre is a machine learning leader and researcher with a background spanning industry and academia.

## Education

He holds a **Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Engineering** from [Universidad Nacional del Sur](https://www.uns.edu.ar/){:target="_blank"} and a **Master of Science (MS) in Computer Science** from the [University of Illinois at Chicago](https://www.uic.edu/){:target="_blank"}, where he studied as a [Fulbright Scholar](https://fulbright.edu.ar/){:target="_blank"}.

## Research Interests

His technical focus is on **machine learning**, **unsupervised learning**, and **video processing**. He has authored a [book and multiple peer-reviewed scientific publications](https://scholar.google.com.ar/citations?hl=en&user=PNNFrAQAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate){:target="_blank"}.

## Professional Experience

Javier currently works as **Director of Machine Learning** at [Teladoc Health](https://www.teladochealth.com/){:target="_blank"}. He also serves as **Adjunct Professor** in the Electronics Engineering Department at [Universidad Tecnológica Nacional, Bahía Blanca](https://www.frbb.utn.edu.ar/frbb/){:target="_blank"}, and has over a decade of experience providing technical consulting to private and public organizations.
